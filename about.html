JANET OUYANG

I am a current fourth-year undergraduate at Simon Fraser University majoring in Interactive Arts and Technology (B.A). Design is a hobby that I have held since middle school, as I ran my own graphic design site for many years. Although my focus is mainly on user experience and interface design, I am also interested in other areas of design, such as game, product, sound, character, fashion, environment, and interior.

My ambition is to help create something large and memorable - something that touches millions across the world.

After all, who wouldn't want to say "I was a part of that!" to the next iPhone, Netflix, Instagram, or even Game of Thrones? I love harmonizing colors, and strive to design as creatively and functionally as possible within any limitations...all while keeping the user in mind, of course.

I am very detail-oriented and greatly enjoy honing little parts of a whole, such as interactions and the placement of particular elements. Meticulous planning and ideating through sketches helps me create projects from my mind's eye.
